Cat's Cradle



Basic Information

Book Name: Cat's Cradle

Author Name: Kurt Vonnegut

Summary: The title of the book derives from the string game "cat's cradle". Early in the book, the character Felix Hoenikker (a fictional co-inventor of the atom bomb) was playing cat's cradle when the bomb was dropped, and the game is later referred to by his son, Newton Hoenikker.
